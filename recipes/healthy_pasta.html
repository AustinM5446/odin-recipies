Healthy Pasta

Description

Love pasta, but trying to eat healthy? Here's a perfect recipie for you!

Ingredients

  • Banzai Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 0 Sodium Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Pot of Water
  2. Put Banzai Pasta in Water
  3. Stir Until Complete
  4. Drain and serve with 0 sodium sauce and parmesan cheese!
