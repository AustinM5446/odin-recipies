Mahi Mahi and Rice

Ingredients

  • Frozen Mahi Mahi
  • Uncle Ben's Whole Grain Rice
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Thaw Mahi Mahi in Refridgerator for 8-12 hours
  2. Cook Mahi Mahi in Pan with Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  3. Cook Uncle Ben's Whole Grain Rice in Microwave
  4. When both are ready, serve together and season!
